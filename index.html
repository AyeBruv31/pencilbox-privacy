Privacy Policy for Pencil Box Games

Last updated: May 2025

Pencil Box Games is a collection of paper-and-pencil games designed for two players sharing one device. We respect your privacy and have built this app to collect as little information as possible.

What we collect

Nothing. Pencil Box Games does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you or your device. The app does not require an internet connection to play.

Data stored on your device

The app saves player names and game results (wins, losses, draws) locally on your device only. This information never leaves your device and is not shared with anyone, including us. You can delete this data at any time by removing players within the app or uninstalling the app.

In-app purchases

Pencil Box Games offers an optional "Tip Jar" with small in-app purchases that support the developer. These purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information. Please refer to Google Play's privacy policy for details on how they handle payment data.

Children's privacy

Pencil Box Games is designed for families and young children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

For users in the UK, EU, and Switzerland

We are aware of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK GDPR, and the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP). Because Pencil Box Games does not collect, store, or process any personal data, most rights under these regulations (such as the right of access, rectification, or erasure) are not applicable — there is no data on our end to access, correct, or delete.

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy in connection with this app, you can contact us at pencilmarkapps@gmail.com.

For users in California

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have certain rights regarding their personal information. Because Pencil Box Games does not collect, sell, or share any personal information, there is no data for us to disclose, delete, or opt out of selling. We do not sell any data, period.

If you have questions, contact us at pencilmarkapps@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the new version at the same web address with an updated "last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, email us at pencilmarkapps@gmail.com.
